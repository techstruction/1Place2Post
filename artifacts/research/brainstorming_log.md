## [2026-03-01] Facebook Business Page Setup

### Objective
Research and define all necessary fields for the 1Place2Post Facebook Business Page to ensure a professional and cohesive brand presence.

### Research Findings
To ensure a Facebook Business Page is professional and "complete" in 2024, the following fields are required or highly recommended:

**Core Identity**
- **Page Name**: official business name.
- **Category**: Up to 3 (e.g., Software Company, Social Media Service).
- **Bio**: Short description (max 255 chars).
- **Username (@handle)**: Unique vanity URL.

**Contact & Connections**
- **Website URL**: official home.
- **Action Button (CTA)**: "Learn More" or "Visit Website".
- **Email/Phone**: Business contact points.

**Brand Assets**
- **Profile Picture**: 180x180 px (Logo focus).
- **Cover Photo**: 820x312 px (Value prop focus).
